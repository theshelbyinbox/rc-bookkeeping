Meet Rachel Cooper

I grew up on the ZX Ranch right here in Oregon. I learned early that nothing runs right if the books don't add up -- whether you're tracking head count or cash flow.

Today, I help business owners across Lake County and beyond get their finances in order so they can stop worrying and start making decisions with real numbers in front of them.

You started your business because you're good at what you do -- whether that's running cattle, building houses, or keeping a shop alive in a small town. The books were never supposed to be the hard part.

When your books are clean, you can see your business clearly for the first time.

That's what I'm here to give you.

What I Handle for You

I handle the monthly bookkeeping -- entering every transaction, reconciling every account, making sure your numbers are right. If you've got employees, I'll run payroll so everyone gets paid correctly and on time. I manage bill payments so nothing falls through the cracks. And when it comes to taxes -- monthly deposits, quarterly filings, year-end W2s and 1099s -- I've got all of it covered.

I even do notary services if you need a document signed.

How much does it cost? That depends on your business, and that's actually a good thing. It means you're only paying for what you need. Most of my clients land somewhere between $200 and $500 a month.

Give me a call and I'll put together a number that makes sense for you.
